=============================================
 Slippi Ranked Stats — Installation Guide
=============================================

WHAT THIS APP DOES
------------------
This app reads your Slippi replay files (.slp) from your computer and shows
you ranked stats like win rate, matchup history, and rating over time.

Your replays never leave your computer. The only internet connection the app
makes is to slippi.gg's own servers — the same ones the slippi.gg website uses
— to fetch your current rating when you ask it to.

Your data is saved to: Documents\Slippi Ranked Stats\ on your PC.


HOW TO INSTALL
--------------
1. Install Python from https://www.python.org/downloads/
   During install, check "Add Python to PATH".
   (You only need to do this once — it takes about 2 minutes.)

2. Extract the Slippi Ranked Stats folder anywhere you like.
   (Recommended: your Desktop or Documents folder)

3. Inside the folder, double-click run.bat
   This sets up the app and creates a "Slippi Ranked Stats" shortcut on your Desktop.

4. From now on, just use the Desktop shortcut to launch the app.
   Your browser will open automatically — no typing needed.

5. In the left sidebar, enter your Slippi connect code (e.g. JOEY#870)
   and point the app at your Slippi replays folder.

That's it.


SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS
-------------------
- Windows 10 or 11
- Python (free, from python.org — see step 1 above)
- Your Slippi replays folder (default: Documents\Slippi\)
- Internet connection (only needed to fetch rating snapshots)


UNINSTALLING
------------
1. Delete the Slippi Ranked Stats folder
2. Delete the Desktop shortcut
3. Optionally delete Documents\Slippi Ranked Stats\ to remove your saved stats


TROUBLESHOOTING
---------------
The app seems to do nothing / browser doesn't open:
  - Wait up to 30 seconds — it loads in the background first
  - Try opening http://localhost:8501 in your browser manually

"Connect code not found" error:
  - Make sure your connect code is in the format XXXX#000 (e.g. JOEY#870)
  - The # symbol is required

Replays aren't showing up:
  - Click "Scan Replays" in the sidebar and point it to your Slippi folder
  - Default location: C:\Users\[YourName]\Documents\Slippi\
